Fawwaz Shabri M
Visual Media Generalist

A curious and driven visual media generalist with a growing portfolio and a strong passion for storytelling through visuals. Since 2020, I have immersed myself in the digital media landscape—learning, experimenting, and collaborating within creative communities. My exploration spans 3D modeling, photography, UI/UX design, branding, and motion graphics, forming a versatile skill set that balances both aesthetics and functionality.

I have had the opportunity to work on freelance projects, intern at NusaEdu, and contribute to the design division of a student organization for over a year—experiences that have sharpened my adaptability, communication skills, and ability to contribute meaningfully within creative teams. My approach is grounded in continuous learning, and I actively explore tools and workflows such as Blender, Figma, and the Adobe Creative Cloud suite.
